I love human anatomy and medical history. One of my most favorite places ever the Mütter Museum. which is part of The College of Physicians of Philadelphia. Some of the many fascinating things you can find there are:

  • The jaw tumor of President Grover Cleveland
  • One of John Wilke Booth's vertebra
  • Sections of Einstein's brain

There are also many exhibits; some permanent, some special (temporary), and even some online! Here are some of them:

Name of Exhibit Permanent, Special, or Online?
The Hyrtl Skull Collection Permanent
Cast of Chang and Eng Bunker Permanent
Grimm's Anatomy: Magic and Medicine Special
Memento Mütter Online
